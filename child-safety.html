Child Safety Standards

Connect maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any form of child exploitation or abuse on our platform.

LAST UPDATED · 2026-03-07

Zero tolerance policy

Connect strictly prohibits any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, or harms minors. This includes but is not limited to:

Prohibited conduct

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Grooming or any attempt to establish inappropriate contact with minors
  • Solicitation or exploitation of minors in any form
  • Sharing content that sexualizes or endangers children
  • Any behavior that facilitates child trafficking or abuse

Safety by design

Connect is designed with minimal data collection and limited interaction features, which inherently reduces risks:

Built-in protections

  • No messaging or chat — users cannot send messages, photos, or any content to each other
  • No user profiles — only a username and a connection counter are stored
  • Physical proximity required — connections only happen via NFC (touching phones), making remote exploitation impossible
  • No personal data — we do not collect names, ages, emails, phone numbers, or locations
  • Anonymous by design — accounts are tied to an anonymous device ID, not to personal identity

Proactive measures

Connect includes a built-in profanity and hate speech filter that prevents inappropriate usernames. The filter covers multiple languages (Spanish, English, Catalan) and detects evasion techniques such as leet speak substitutions and character obfuscation.

Since Connect does not allow users to exchange messages, images, links, or any form of content, the attack surface for grooming, exploitation, or CSAM distribution is effectively zero.

Users can permanently delete all their data at any time using the in-app RESET button. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Response process

ENFORCEMENT

  • Law enforcement — we report all instances of CSAM and child exploitation to the relevant authorities, including NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children) and local law enforcement agencies
  • Full cooperation — we cooperate with law enforcement investigations related to child safety

Legal compliance

Connect complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — provisions for minors
  • Google Play Developer Policy on Child Safety
  • Applicable national and regional child protection laws
